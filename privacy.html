Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Last updated: January 2026 · This is placeholder content for demonstration and should be reviewed before going live.

1. Introduction

Dynamic Roofing Canterbury Limited ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use and protect your personal information in line with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide when you contact us or request a quote, including:

  • Your name and contact details (phone, email, address).
  • Details about your property and the roofing work you need.
  • Any feedback you choose to share with us.

3. How we use your information

We use your information to respond to enquiries, prepare quotes, carry out and manage roofing work, issue invoices, and improve our service. We will not use your information for unrelated purposes without your consent.

4. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with trusted parties who help us deliver our services (such as suppliers or subcontractors) only where necessary, and with any authority where required by law.

5. Feedback submissions

When you submit private feedback through our website, that information is sent directly to us so we can respond. It is not published publicly without your permission.

6. Storage and security

We take reasonable steps to keep your information secure and to protect it from loss, misuse or unauthorised access. We retain personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described, or as required by law.

7. Your rights

You have the right to request access to, and correction of, the personal information we hold about you. To make a request, please get in touch using the details below.

8. Contact us

For any privacy questions, call 021 0255 6680 or email info@dynamicroofingcanterbury.co.nz.
