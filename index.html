What we do

Roofing done properly, from ridge to gutter.

Whether it's a full re-roof, a stubborn leak or a brand-new build, we handle the lot — and we tidy up after ourselves.

01

Re-Roofing & Replacement

Tired, leaking or storm-damaged roof? We strip it back and replace it with new long-run or tile — with minimal disruption.

02

New Roofs

Building or extending? We work in with your builder to get your new roof watertight, straight and finished on schedule.

03

Roof Repairs & Leaks

Water where it shouldn't be? We find the source, fix it fast and check the rest of the roof while we're up there.

04

Long-Run Metal Roofing

Coloursteel and long-run iron cut to length and laid clean — the tough, low-fuss choice for Canterbury homes.

05

Spouting & Gutters

New spouting, downpipes and continuous guttering — sized and fitted to move Canterbury rain off your roof for good.

06

Roof Inspections & Reports

Buying, selling or just not sure? We'll get up on the roof, take a proper look and give you an honest rundown of its condition.
